Learning Plan

My long term goal is to get a job where I can build a career, where I can be able to work from home or office, or a cafe down the road.

To be able to work under pressure while working under a deadline and being able to to the job properly.

My strengths are that I'm loyal, honest and empathetic; I’m able to place myself in other people’s place and try to see things their perspective; I have a good sense of humour, I'm good working in teams as much as by myself. As my limitations, I sometimes doubt myself and need some sort of reassurance that what I'm doing is right.

In order for me to work productively, I'm working on getting used to the 'timebox', and also by using 'toggl' I am more aware that 'the time is running' and this helps me focus and stop me from procrastinating; I'm building a routine around my study times and this allows me to have time walk my dog and use this time to reflect on what I have learn.

Whenever I need help, I will use 'slack' to ask a question, depending on the case, either directly to my facilitator or to other learners, as well as if I see that someone needs help and it is in my possibilities, I also would help others this same way.

To point me in the right direction if I need some correction. I also would expect them to give me real world examples from their experiences.
