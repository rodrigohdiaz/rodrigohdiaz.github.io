Born and raised in Guatemala, now a NZ citizen; I have been living in Auckland since 2011.

My diverse jobs have ranged from flight attendant, hospitality, labourer, security for an international airline, planning technician, draughts person, design consultant and architect to get me where I am now. Each of these have given me different experience, all of which is valuable in different situations in any work and try to bring this experience to any situation in my life.

My passions include photography (35mm/120mm film and digital) my favourite type of photography being street photography, but I capture a moment or a detail, a closeup or a panorama, in black and white or colour; I like video games and read.
